## Ingredients

- {{<c>}}1 cup of banana flour
- {{<c>}}1 mashed banana
- {{<c>}}⅓ cup flour
- {{<c>}}2 tsp baking powder
- {{<c>}}1 tsp vanilla essense
- {{<c>}}⅓ brown sugar
- {{<c>}}2 eggs (beaten)
- {{<c>}}1½ oat milk

## Prep

Whisk all the ingredients together.

Heat a pan. Put grease/oil/butter in pan. Cook pancakes.
