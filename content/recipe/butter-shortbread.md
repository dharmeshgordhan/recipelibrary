## Ingredients

- {{<c>}}500g butter
- {{<c>}}1 cup icing sugar
- {{<c>}}1 cup cornflour
- {{<c>}}3 cups flour
- {{<c>}}1 tsp baking powder

### Optional mix-ins

- cinnamon
- cardamon
- lemon zest
- cocoa powder

## Prep

Cream butter and sugar together til pale and fluffy.

Mix in the cornflour and flour in batches.

Roll the shortbread into 4-6 logs.

Refridgerate for up to 3 days.

Preheat oven to 150C.

Bake for 20-30 mins.
