Black Rice & Edamame Salad

December 6, 2020
prep 20mins
cook 45mins
rest 120mins
serves 8

credit - Amelia

Ingredients#

  • 1 cup raw black rice
  • 1 packet frozen Edamame soya shelled beans
  • 1 cup currants
  • ½ cup pinenuts - roasted (optional)

Dressing#

  • 3 tbsp sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp soya sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic - crushed
  • 1 tsp ginger - grated
  • 4 kaffir leaves - finely chopped
  • 1 red chilli seeds removed- finely chopped
  • black pepper

Prep#

Cook rice until nutty texture; don’t overcook. Drain and cool.

Mix dressing together.

Combine all ingredients. let rest for two hours.

Optional: can add baked chicken or some other meat.
