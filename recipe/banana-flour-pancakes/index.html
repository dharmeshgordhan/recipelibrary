Banana Flour Pancakes

December 6, 2020

Adapted from the back of some packet somewhere

Ingredients#

  • 1 cup of banana flour
  • 1 mashed banana
  • ⅓ cup flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla essense
  • ⅓ brown sugar
  • 2 eggs (beaten)
  • 1½ oat milk

Prep#

Whisk all the ingredients together.

Heat a pan. Put grease/oil/butter in pan. Cook pancakes.
