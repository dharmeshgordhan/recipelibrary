    Butter Shortbread

    December 7, 2020

    Ingredients#

    • 500g butter
    • 1 cup icing sugar
    • 1 cup cornflour
    • 3 cups flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder

    Optional mix-ins#

    • cinnamon
    • cardamon
    • lemon zest
    • cocoa powder

    Prep#

    Cream butter and sugar together til pale and fluffy.

    Mix in the cornflour and flour in batches.

    Roll the shortbread into 4-6 logs.

    Refridgerate for up to 3 days.

    Preheat oven to 150C.

    Bake for 20-30 mins.
